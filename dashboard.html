Project Overview

This interactive dashboard provides a deep dive into the socio-economic development of major world economies from 1960 to 2018. It visualizes the correlation between economic growth, digital adoption, and quality of life across different regions.

Technical Process

  • Data Reshaping: Transformed complex World Bank "Wide Format" data into "Long Format" using Pandas melt() for time-series analysis.
  • Visualization: Built a multi-chart interactive layout using Plotly to allow side-by-side country comparisons.
  • AI Integration: Implemented a Linear Regression model to forecast GDP trends up to 2030 based on historical slopes.

Key Insights

Digital Leapfrogging: India and China show a sharp exponential curve in internet adoption post-2005, directly impacting their service sector GDP.


Quality of Life: Despite lower GDP per capita, Life Expectancy in developing nations is closing the gap with developed nations at a 40% faster rate.
